What is MediConnect ?

MediConnect is open platform to medical service providers and service consumers. By using this plataform, Service providers can manage their customer relationship and customers can manage their health related information on it. They can communicate each other.

Why MediConnect ?

MediConnect is free and simple to use. its provide all functionality which are required to every health service providres and service consumers. also it can be accessed from anywhere and form any device.
